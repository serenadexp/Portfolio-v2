The Concept

The idea came from actually working out on the bench press at a local gym while trying to figure out a project that me as an individual could enjoy doing. So I like exercise and I wanted to see how I could bring this hobby of mine and stick it into the sandwich that we all call the web.

Design

I knew I’d want a site that gives off a clean open feel that doesn't make the user feel too cluttered and easy on the eyes. That's what I was shooting for.

Development

This site is powered by the CodeIgniter framework and pulls data in and out of the database to bring videos the user has chosen.
